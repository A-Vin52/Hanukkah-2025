# חג חנוכה שמח

## User Guide

**Important:**  
This application works **only during the days of Hanukkah — 14–21 December 2025**.

### How to Use the App

1. **Read the blessings** displayed on the screen.
2. **Click on the Shamash candle** to light it.
3. After a few seconds, the lit Shamash will **tilt automatically**.
4. **Click and drag the tilted Shamash** toward the candles to light them.
5. **Release the Shamash** — it will return to its place automatically.
6. After lighting the candles, **wait for your surprise** — each day reveals a **new surprise**.

---

Enjoy the experience, and **חג חנוכה שמח!**

**PS** In case you did any mistake during light of the candles, you need click "Reset Menorah" button and after click OK. And repeat lighting.
